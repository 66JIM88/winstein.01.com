Game Overview 游戏概览

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating increasingly difficult enemies. With intuitive controls and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels.

Monster Survivors 是一款充满动作元素的生存游戏，你将面对无尽的怪物浪潮。 你的目标很简单：尽可能长时间地生存，同时收集能力提升并击败越来越难对付的敌人。 凭借直观的控制和令人上瘾的游戏玩法，Monster Survivors 为各种技能水平的游戏玩家提供了数小时的娱乐。

How to Play 如何游玩

Tips and Strategies 技巧和策略

To maximize your survival time in Monster Survivors, focus on creating a balanced build of abilities. Prioritize movement speed early on to help dodge enemies, then focus on increasing your attack power and range. Don't stay in one place for too long – keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Health regeneration abilities can be crucial for long-term survival, so grab those when available!

为了最大限度地延长你在Monster Survivors中的生存时间，专注于创建均衡的能力构建。 在早期优先提高移动速度以帮助躲避敌人，然后专注于提高你的攻击力和攻击范围。 不要在一个地方停留太久 - 保持移动以避免被包围。 生命值再生能力对长期生存至关重要，所以在可用时一定要获取这些能力！
